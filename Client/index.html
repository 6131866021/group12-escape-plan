Game Instructions

There are 2 characters in the games; Astronaut and Alien

The player who become the Alien will start to move first.

All players cannot access the obstacle blocks (UFO) and only the astronaut can access the rocket block.

For each turn, you will have 10 seconds to move to one adjacent blocks.

If the alien accesses to the same block of the astronaut, the player who plays the alien will win.

But if the astronaut accesses the rocket, the player who plays the astronaut will win.
